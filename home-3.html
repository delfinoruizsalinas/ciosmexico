About Our Event

Eventy Festival gathers world famous groups, DJs, and performers at a single place for you to enjoy the best music.

With 5 stages dedicated to various music genres that include Rock, Indie, Hip-Hop, EDM, and Bass music, our festival can offer you an unprecedented lineup of artists you’ve probably only dreamt of.
